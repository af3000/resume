About Me

Focus on AI Ethics and Privacy: Keen interest in federated machine learning and Privacy Enhancing Technologies (PETs), with a commitment to enhancing privacy and security in data handling. 

 

Government Sector Experience: Over 10 years of experience as a Business Analyst/Product Manager at Ontario's GovTech (IT Cluster), working with policy experts, communications, and engineering teams to deliver applications affecting millions in sectors like Child Care and Career Colleges.

 

Advocacy and Policy Insight: Collaborated with leadership at a Web3 advocacy group to research digital currency policies impacting stablecoins and regulations in the Canadian market.

 

User Acquisition and Platform Engagement: Worked with the CTO and CEO of an automated crypto trading platform, successfully onboarding users via platforms like X and Discord.

 

Entrepreneurship: Founded BakeNinja, a startup that evolved from concept to a thriving decentralized marketplace for local bakers, incubated at the Founder Institute by Adeo Ressi. This led to one of Instagram's most recognized baking hashtags.

 

Innovation Enthusiast: Passionate about staying at the forefront of emerging technologies, eager to connect for collaborative ventures or career opportunities to drive innovation, safety and usefulness in Web3 and AI.

 

Education: I hold a master's in Information Studies from University of Toronto and specialized in Knowledge Management, Human-Centred Design and Information Systems Design working with the following notable faculty:

  • Professor Eric Yu (pioneered the social modeling i* framework).
  • Professor Wei Choo (knowledge management and decision making).
  • Professor Andrew Clement (human-centred design, privacy and identity).
