About Me

I am an experienced product designer and developer relations expert with a passion for Web3 and blockchain technology. My unique blend of policy insight and technical skills has allowed me to collaborate with leadership at a Web3 advocacy group, where I've focused on gathering research on digital currency policies and regulations within the Canadian market. Additionally, I've worked alongside the CTO and CEO of an automated crypto trading platform, where I successfully acquired and onboarded users through platforms like X and Discord.

My career includes a tenure as a Business Analyst/Product Manager at Ontario's GovTech (IT Cluster), where I collaborated with policy experts, communications teams and software engineering teams to deliver applications impacting millions in sectors ranging from Child Care to Career Colleges.

I also founded BakeNinja, a startup from ideation to execution, creating a brand, community, and marketplace for a decentralized network of local bakers. This venture was incubated at the Founder Institute, established by Adeo Ressi, and lead to the creation of one of Instagram's most recognized hashtags in the baking community.

I'm passionate about staying at the forefront of emerging technologies and am excited to connect with like-minded individuals for potential collaborations or employment opportunities. With my blend of technical skills, policy insight, and entrepreneurial spirit, I'm poised to drive innovation in Web3 and beyond.
