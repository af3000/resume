About Me

I am an experienced product designer and developer relations expert with a passion for Web3 and blockchain technology. My unique blend of policy insight and technical skills has allowed me to collaborate with leadership at a Web3 advocacy group, where I've focused on gathering and disseminating research on digital currency policies and regulations within the Canadian market. Additionally, I've worked alongside the CTO and CEO of an automated crypto trading platform, where I successfully acquired and onboarded users through platforms like X and Discord.

Over a decade, I've refined my design skills within various software development environments, from startup incubators, where I recruited global coding talent and built brand identity from the ground up, to a senior product management role in a large government agency. Here, I worked alongside policy analysts, communications teams, and software engineering and DevOps professionals. My contributions include:

  • Maintenance of extensive technical specifications and business documentation for diverse projects, ranging from Archaeology to Child Care.
  • Innovation in user experience through user workshops and ongoing retrospectives/feedback.
  • Support and leadership for onboarding and post-production via training materials, video tutorials, and user manuals.

I also founded a startup from ideation to execution, creating a brand, community, and marketplace for a decentralized network of local bakers. This venture was incubated at the Founder Institute, established by Adeo Ressi, leading to the creation of one of Instagram's most recognized hashtags in the baking community.

I am continually expanding my knowledge in emerging technologies and am eager to connect with like-minded individuals for potential employment and/or collaboration opportunities.
