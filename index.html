About Me

I’m a Product Manager with a proven track record of delivering projects and simplifying complex systems. I’m currently shaping user experiences at PublicAI, a San Francisco-based AI startup revolutionizing the AI ecosystem by delivering premium, on-demand AI training data while empowering individuals worldwide to monetize their expertise using crypto. I also volunteer with the Create the Future Summit, fostering a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) that connects Web3 innovators and social impact leaders to co-create a permissionless future.

Passionate about Web3 and tokenomics, I’m eager to collaborate on projects leveraging smart contracts, NFTs, or DeFi protocols with visionary teams building on Ethereum or other blockchains. Here's a brief summary about me:

 

Web3 Product Design & Community:
